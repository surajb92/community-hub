Connect4

  • Turn-based color matching game.
  • Drop your coin color from top alternatively (hint will be shown on hover/touch-hold).
  • First player to form 4 coins of their color - either in a horizontal/vertical line or diagonally - wins.

Typ Wars

  • Real-time speed-typing game.
  • Words will drop from top periodically, type them in the bottom text box & press Enter to clear them.
  • 10 points for each word cleared.
  • If you've partially typed a word, it will be highlighted until where you have typed.
  • Words reaching the bottom of your game screen loses you one life.
  • If you lose all 3 lives, the game ends.

    • 2-player mode specific rules

  • Clearing existing words gives you only 2 points instead of the regular 10.
  • To get the regular 10 points, you should send dictionary words to your opponent using the bottom text box.
  • Losing a life also loses you 100 score.
  • The player to lose all their lives first loses the game, even if they have higher score.
